🐂 Overview

THE BULL HORN is a one‑click 3D action game for your browser with a meme‑coin identity, $MESNA. You play a shape‑shifting beast — charge as a bull, transform into a minotaur — across three escalating realms: a walkable adventure, a breakneck runner, and an ice‑and‑fire boss arena.

No sign‑up wall: press Play and you're in as a guest. Optionally connect a Phantom wallet to claim your victory reward. Everything runs in the browser (Three.js) on ordinary shared hosting — no downloads, no installs.

🚀 Quick Start

  1. Open the game and press Play.
  2. Choose Connect Phantom or Play as Guest (guest is instant).
  3. Walk, fight and clear Level 1 → run Level 2 → survive Level 3.
  4. Beat Level 3 to unlock the victory reward claim.

🗺️ The Three Realms

One continuous journey, three very different games:

I · Adventure

🌲 The Realm

Walk the road through five themed towns. Fight foes, collect gold, earn XP & levels, buy potions, and fell the Rust King.

II · The Run

🏃 The Gauntlet

Auto‑run a 3‑lane track. Dodge, jump, slide and smash crates, and outrun The Devourer to the gate.

III · Ice & Fire

🔥❄ Punk Hazard

A split ice/fire arena. Use transform + melee + fire‑shooting to survive 6 waves of big monsters and the Ashen Warden boss.

🔄 The Beast — Two Forms

Swap forms at any time (F / the 🔄 button). Each fights differently:

🐂 Bull

Quadruped bison. Gore‑charge — dash forward and ram enemies with your horns. Fast and heavy‑hitting.

👊 Minotaur

Upright brute. Punch / kick / axe combos and a higher jump. In Level 3 it also wields the fire‑shooting skill.

🎁 Rewards & Wallet

  • Connecting is read‑only (address only) — no transaction signing to play.
The claim flow connects your wallet and confirms the reward. On‑chain token settlement goes live alongside the $MESNA contract.

❓ FAQ

Do I need a wallet to play?

No. Press Play → Play as Guest and start instantly. A wallet is only needed to claim the Level 3 reward.

Which wallet is supported?

Phantom (Solana). Connecting is address‑only — you never sign a transaction just to play.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes — controls, HUD and layout are touch‑friendly with an on‑screen joystick and action buttons.

Is my progress saved?

Level 1 progress saves to an anonymous guest tied to your browser (no password).

Where's the contract address?

It appears on the home page (with a copy button) and above, once the token is live.
